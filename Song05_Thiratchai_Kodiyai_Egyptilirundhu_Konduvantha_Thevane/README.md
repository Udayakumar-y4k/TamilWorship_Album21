Psalm 80 – Israel pictured as a vine brought out of Egypt, planted and spreading, then broken by enemies. Plea for God to restore and shine His face again. Points to Christ as the true Vine (John 15:1).
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ, true Vine,
You brought us out of Egypt and planted us.
Restore what the enemy has broken.
Shine Your face upon us and make us fruitful.
Come quickly.
